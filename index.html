ABOUT US SERVICES

FULFILL YOUR POTENTIAL

Cozza Coaching caters to a wide range of athletes. Whether your goal is to get stronger, gain muscle or lose weight, you're in safe hands.

Engagement in sports has been a lifelong journey for me. Prior to Powerlifting, I participated in football, athletics and gymnastics competitively. I also studied Exercise & Health at University, where I earned my level 2 Fitness Instructor and level 3 Personal Trainer qualifications.

I have always enjoyed helping people achieve their goals. With my formal education and personal experiences combined, I believe that I have a great chance of achieving this through coaching.

OUR SERVICES

PERSONALISED PROGRAMS

Get a fully personalised training program catered to your goals. We offer programming for both hypertrophy and strength!

  • Consultation to discuss goals, schedule and equipment availability.
  • Three fully personalised 4 week training blocks (12 weeks in total)
  • Repeatable program (input your new SBD numbers after each cycle)
  • Program revisions made to ensure that you are happy.
GET STARTED

1-2-1 ONLINE COACHING

High contact powerlifting coaching with weekly programming adjustments and 24/7 Whatsapp contact for technical feedback & advice.

  • Initial consultation to discuss goals and determine whether we're a good fit.
  • Weekly programming updates via google sheets.
  • Weekly round up and brief for subsequent weeks.
  • 24/7 access to me via whatsapp.
  • Meetday handling, where possible.
GET STARTED

MEET DAY HANDLING

Ensure that your meet goes smoothly with the help of an experienced gameday handler, who has a proven track record.

  • For self-programmed athletes, I can devise a meetday plan based on your goals and competition/training history.
  • For athletes who have a coach, I can help you execute the plan that they provide.
  • Ensure that you are in the right place at the right time.
  • Ensure than warmups, food & fluid consumption are adequately paced.
